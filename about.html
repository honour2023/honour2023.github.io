Brief Insight

I was born in the 20th century in the largest city in Sub-saharan Africa, Ibadan. I had my early education at Royal Nursery and Primary School before I went to Ibadan City Academy. Afterwards I proceeded to the highly revered Ladoke Akintola University of Technology,where I obtained my Bachelors of Technology in Computer Engineering.While I was in school, I participated in several socio-political activities which was crowned by my emergence as the first President of Oyo State Students Union. After my National Service I embarked on a research in Advance Wireless Communications at Convergence Labs, Hyderabad, India. I came back to Nigeria to impact my Knowledge on the society and here I am riding on the platform of MEST to make that happen.
